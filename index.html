President (May 2020 - Present):

I joined this organization because I believe and admire SASE's mission:

  • - Prepare Asian heritage scientists and engineers for success in the global business world.
  • - Celebrate diversity on campuses and in the workplace.
  • - Provide opportunities for members to make contributions to their local communities.
As the President of the Cincinnati chapter of the Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers, I lead a cabinet of five executive officers and hold biwekly board meetings to ensure there is efficient delegation of duties. During my time as President, I have been able to increase our student membership by 21% by leading effective general body meetings. Our general body meetings are designed to encourage Asian heritage students communicate with other engineers and provide resources for students to find internships and professional opportunities. By joining this organization, I have been able to network with other scientists and engineers from all over the country. I have learned there is a supportive Asian-heritage community of scientists and engineers that are dedicated to helping one another grow personally and professionally. I've learned how to communicate with a variety of people, and understand the importance of minorities in the workplace.
